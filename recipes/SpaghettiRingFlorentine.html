Spaghetti Ring Florentine

A delightful and visually appealing dish featuring a savory blend of spaghetti, spinach, cheese, and eggs, molded into a ring shape and baked to perfection.

Perfect for a unique and tasty addition to any meal.

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti, cooked and drained
  • 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 package (10 ounces) frozen chopped spinach, thawed and well drained
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt butter or margarine over medium heat. Stir in flour, salt, and pepper until well blended.
  3. Gradually add milk, cooking and stirring constantly until the mixture thickens and comes to a boil. Remove from heat.
  4. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the cooked spaghetti and beaten eggs.
  6. Add the cheese sauce to the spaghetti mixture and mix well.
  7. Fold in the well-drained spinach.
  8. Pour the mixture into a greased 9-inch ring mold or Bundt pan.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese evenly on top.
  10. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 30 minutes or until the top is lightly browned and the mixture is set.
  11. Allow to cool for a few minutes, then carefully unmold onto a serving platter.
