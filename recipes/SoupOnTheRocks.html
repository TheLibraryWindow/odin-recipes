Soup On The Rocks

A classic and comforting soup served with a twist, inspired by a nostalgic Campbell’s Soup advertisement from 1956.

Perfect for warming up on chilly days or as a comforting starter.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10 1/2 ounces) Campbell’s Condensed Soup (your choice of flavor)
  • 1/2 cup milk (optional, for creamier texture)
  • Crackers or breadsticks, for serving

Steps

  1. Heat Campbell’s Condensed Soup in a saucepan over medium heat until hot, stirring occasionally.
  2. If desired, stir in milk to achieve a creamier consistency.
  3. Once heated through, carefully pour the soup into a heat-resistant glass or mug.
  4. Serve immediately with crackers or breadsticks on the side.
